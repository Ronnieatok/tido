About Us

Rooted in Turkana, driven by inclusion

Turkana Inclusive Development Organization (TIDO) is a community-based organization established to champion inclusive, sustainable development across Turkana County. We exist because too many children, youth, women, and persons with disabilities still face barriers to education, livelihoods, health care, and meaningful participation in the decisions that shape their lives.

We work hand-in-hand with local communities, government, and partners to break down those barriers — through grassroots programs, advocacy, and a firm belief that lasting change must be community-owned. Every initiative we run is guided by our constitution and steered by members who call Turkana home.

Our Vision

To build an inclusive, empowered, and self-reliant Turkana community where all people, including children, youth, women, and persons with disabilities, have equal opportunities to thrive and participate in sustainable development.

Our Mission

To promote inclusive development through empowering children, youth, women, and persons with disabilities by enhancing access to education, livelihood opportunities, health services, advocacy, and community participation in Turkana County.
